## Note on edition and recency

Where multiple editions of a work exist, the most recent edition has been used in writing the case study. Statutory references are to the public law as enacted; subsequent amendments are noted in the country deep-dives where they are load-bearing for the argument. The bibliography is intentionally working-length rather than exhaustive; a researcher building on this case study should treat it as a starting point for their own literature search.
